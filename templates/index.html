About Us

No Farmer, No Food, No Future!

Agriculture is of primary importance to the Indian economy and Indian Farmers are aging. Both young & middle age people are avoiding agriculture. Over the past few years, rural youth have been shying away from agriculture.

This has several implications for the future of Indian agriculture and India’s food security. Rural youth can play an important role in ensuring food security if they are encouraged to involve in farming and the challenges they face are addressed.

Increased involvement of youth in agricultural activities will help reduce the problems of the ageing farm population and increasing youth employment.

We are Encouraging Youth Involvement in Agriculture and Agribusiness.

Our Solutions

Together with our partners, we are creating digital solutions to make the next generation of farmers to be self dependent.

FarmSocial

FarmSocial is an Social initiative. Through FarmSocial, farmers can connect with one another to solve problems, share ideas and spread innovation which helps farmers to produce a higher quality product, increase yields, gain insight into marketing pricing, tackle the effects of climate change, source the best seeds, fertiliser and loans, diversify agricultural interests, and much more.

FarmVentory / FarmCloud

FarmVentory is a technology platform where Farmers can list their produce and sell directly to the end buyers. Farmers can manage their inventory online and connect directly with the buyers and logistics partners.

FarmPloyee

We are encouraging young farmers and youth. Providing them linkage both in Local & Global markets, technology solutions, knowledge, we are trying to convert Farmers into Agripreneur. We are working to make farming as a micro entrepreneurship opportunity & stop farmers saying, “we do this because we have no choice”.
